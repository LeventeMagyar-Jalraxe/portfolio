My works explore the intersection of nature, symbolism, human existence, and technological futures. Combining traditional drawing techniques with digital post-processing, I create highly detailed, narrative artworks.

Guardians of Light

This series showcases critically endangered species through unique portraits woven from threads of light. My goal is to raise awareness for wildlife conservation and contribute to the protection of these magnificent species.


The portraits are originally drawn on 30×40 cm Clairefontaine Pastelmat paper using Faber-Castell Polychromos pencils. After digitization, they receive their final, brilliant luminescence using Autodesk Sketchbook.

Pygmy Three-Toed Sloth (Bradypus pygmaeus)

The first featured species: This charming, cat-sized little creature is one of the rarest mammals on Earth — only about 100 individuals remain, living exclusively on a tiny island.

Tapanuli Orangutan (Pongo tapanuliensis)

The rarest great ape species on Earth, with fewer than 800 individuals remaining in the wild.

Andean mountain cat (Leopardus jacobita)

The Andean mountain cat is one of the rarest and least known wild cat species in the world. It is estimated that fewer than 1,500 individuals remain in the wild, living in isolated populations across the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

Chinese Pangolin (Manis pentadactyla)

Meet the next Guardian of Light: the Chinese pangolin (Manis pentadactyla). The only mammal wrapped in hard, protective scales — once nature’s perfect armor. Today, it can’t protect them from humans. Poaching and illegal trade make them one of the most endangered and trafficked mammals on Earth.

About Me

The artist behind the pseudonym Jalraxe.

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Magyar Levente (Jalraxe), a visual artist specializing in combining traditional hand-drawn techniques with modern digital tools.

My artistic journey focuses on capturing deep human emotions, environmental issues, and striking contrasts using rich details and unique light effects. Every piece tells a unique story waiting to be discovered.
