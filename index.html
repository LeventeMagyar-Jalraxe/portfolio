About Me

The artist behind the pseudonym Jalraxe.

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Magyar Levente (Jalraxe), a visual artist specializing in combining traditional hand-drawn techniques with modern digital tools.

My artistic journey focuses on capturing deep human emotions, environmental issues, and striking contrasts using rich details and unique light effects. Every piece tells a unique story waiting to be discovered.
